Shanghai, China

I am the founder and PI of the IntelLiSim Lab at John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science, School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interests lie in computer graphics, physical simulation, and physics-based intelligence.

I am a tenure-track associate professor at SJTU and am looking for motivated students to join my group. If you are excited about computer graphics, physical simulation, and physics-based intelligence and plan on applying to postdoc or PhD programs, please reach out.

Before joining SJTU, I was a senior research scientist at Tencent Shanghai, LightSpeed Studios, working with Kui Wu. Previously, I was a Postdoc at Inria Saclay, working with Prof. Mathieu Desbrun. I obtained my doctorate from the School of Information Science & Technology of ShanghaiTech University, supervised by Prof. Xiaopei Liu, focusing on fluid simulations.

I received my B.S. degree in computer science from Northwestern Polytechnical University, China, supervised by Prof. Yong Xia.

We are looking for highly motivated students or researchers to join IntelLiSim Lab, including:

  1. One Postdoc;
  2. Visiting students;
  3. Research Assistants.
